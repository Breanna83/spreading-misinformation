The Dangers of Spreading Misinformation

It's like a really bad game of telephone

Our animation is about the dangers of spreading misinformation. What happens is our main character watches a show in which Bad representation of people with Dissociative identity disorder (DID) is shown. She then goes to tell her friend about this adding her own interpretation of it which results in more misinformation being spread. That friend is coincidentally part of a big podcast which millions of people listen to. While talking about it, they add there own assumptions in that are also wrong about the community. These millions of people know wrong information about a situation. Now lets say just one of those people goes and tell this to all of their friends? This is how a very hurtful cycle is born.
