The Dangers of Spreading Misinformation

It's like a really bad game of telephone

My animation is about the dangers of spreading misinformation. What happes is our main character watches a show in which bad representation of people with Dissociative identity disorder (DID) is shown. She then goes to tell her friend about this adding her own interpertaion of it which results in more misinformation beign spread. That friend is coincidntally part of a big podcast which millions of people listen to. While talking about it, they add there own assumptions in that are also wrong about the community. Thse millions of people know wrong information about a situation. Now lets say just one of those people goes and tell this to all of their friends? This is how a very hurtful cycle is born.
